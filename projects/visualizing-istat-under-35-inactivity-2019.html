Visualizing ISTAT

In 2019, the inactive people aged under 35 in Italy were more than 6 millions. Who are they? And why they are so?

About

The visualization shows the status (employed, unemployed or inactive) of the people aged under 35 in Italy considering their gender, age and highest level of education according to ISTAT 2019 data. It also shows how the inactivity rate has changed over the past 15 years and the main reasons of it, divided by gender and age.
